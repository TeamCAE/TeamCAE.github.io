OUR HISTORY

In this modern world, time demands the importance of the most valued entity. Our project - Automatic Electricity Bill Generator-cum-Tracker is an exceedingly time conservative useful thought if made into reality, we can eliminate the need for a professional and systematic regular check of the electrical reading in the power utilization meter fixed at our residences. This system autonomously accounts the once-a-month usage of electricity and directs the bill to the user via the electrical board of the city.
